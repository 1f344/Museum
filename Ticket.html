Parking

Please check Metro.net for a list of local parking lots. From the Regional Connector Transit Project page, in the sidebar menu, under “Maps,” click on “Little Tokyo Parking Facilities” to view a list of locations.

directions_bus

Public Transportation

Metro Gold Line Take the Gold Line to the Little Tokyo/Arts District stop. We are located just across the street! For information about schedule and fares, use the Metro trip planner at www.metro.net or call (800)COMMUTE.

hotel

HOTEL

MIYAKO HOTEL Visiting from out of town? Stay at the Miyako Hotel located nearby and take advantage of a special rate for Museum supporters.
